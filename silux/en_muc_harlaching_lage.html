Munich Harlaching, Harthauser Str. 9

Location: The apartment is located in the district Harlaching in one of the most popular residential areas in the south of Munich. Its proximity to the river Isar, the famous restaurant Menterschwaige and other green areas give a high leisure and recreational value to the area.

The Munich Marienplatz can be reached by public transport in about 20 minutes. The nearest tram stop, Theodolindenplatz, is 3 minutes and the closest bus stop, Seybothstraße, 5 minutes away. Restaurants, medical practices and shops for daily needs can be found within walking distance.
