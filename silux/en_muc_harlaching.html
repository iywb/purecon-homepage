Munich Harlaching, Harthauser Str. 9

Facilities: The 80 sq.m apartment is fully equipped to high standards. Luxury facilities include a new, modern fitted kitchen with very high quality equipment. Kitchen and bathroom are laid with exquisite floor tiles and electric underfloor heating. The bathroom with window also has an exclusive custom built vanity unit, a very modern detached shower / bath combination and high quality sanitary fittings.

There is parquet flooring in the living room which is equipped with a leather sofa with sleeping function, a flat screen TV (including pay TV), a first class stereo system and a designer dining table and chairs. In the modern office, the bedroom and the corridor have well designed integrated cupboards. The apartment has two covered balconies, which are in a quiet location surrounded by greenery. Broadband Internet access is provided at no extra charge.
